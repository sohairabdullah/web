Writings, Research, and Past Work: Papers, projects, and book chapters.


  • Computer Architecture.

    Developing a power loss protection circuit for cache flushing in NAND hard drives in Axellio edge computing systems. The architecture ensures that data being written onto the NAND hard-drive in a write back cache mode will save the data currently in cache by allowing it sufficient current/energy to flush itself onto M.2 solid state drive.

  • Numerical Analysis.

    Developing Simpson's double integral algorithm to visualize volume integrals of functions that cannot be computed analytically. The code used is available at this github repository.

  • Complex Analysis:

    Obtaining a family of harmonic mappings on the unit disk whose images are rotationally symmetric rosettes with n vertices, n greater than two. A family of harmonic mappings is analyzed. In particular, for a four sided rosette mapping, we obtain an embedded triply periodic minimal surface is obtained.

    Considers the feasibility of using a magnetic field to confine the trajectories of dispelled ions from an electric ion thruster.   • Mathematical Statistics:

  • Multihop High Frequency Radio Wave propagation:

    Models the impact of seawater turbulence on radio wave transmittance, by analying interaction of electromagnetic radiation with the earth's seas or atmosphere (ionosphere). This research paper was part of Contest in Mathematical Modelling, of which Jack Schrott, Nick Crews, and I won an honorable mention. This paper is also the basis of my undergraduate physics thesis.
